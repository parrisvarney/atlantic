Atlantic Engineering Associates, Inc. has been providing top quality service in Massachusetts for over 30 years. We utilize the latest technological equipment and software which allows us to serve our clients in the most efficient and accurate manner. We strongly belive in being affordable and reasonable in our pricing. By maintaining low overhead costs; we are able to pass the savings to out clients. We serve a wide range of clients including government, municipal commercial and residential.

Our field and office staff have substantial professional experience and training. Safety is a priority for us whereby our employees are OSHA certified, MBTA Right of Way Certified and MBCR Railroad Safety Certified. We pride in our values on education and encourage all employees to continue their educational by offering tuition reimbursement.

We are proud to be members of the Massachusetts Association of Land Surveyors and Engineers and the National Society of Professional Engineers.

We are Pre-qualified with the Massachusetts Department of Transportation.

We are a Woman owned business.
