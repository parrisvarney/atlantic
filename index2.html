Atlantic Engineering Associates, Inc. has been providing top quality land surveying and engineering services in Massachusetts for over 30 years. We utilize the latest technological equipment and software which allows us to serve our clients in the most efficient and accurate manner. We strongly believe in being honest and reasonable in our pricing. By maintaining low overhead costs; we are able to pass the savings on to our clients. We are fully licensed and insured.

Atlantic serves a wide range of clients including Government, Municipal, Commercial and Residential. Five of our survey crews are prequalified by Mass DOT for Engineering Field Survey Services. Atlantic has 20 years experience on bridges and highways under contract with the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. Atlantic is also prequalified by their Architects and Engineers Review Board.

We can proudly say, that in 32 years of business, we have never failed to complete work, never had a contract terminated nor have we ever been involved in a lawsuit filed against us.

Because Safety is of utmost importance to us; all of our team is OSHA certified, MBTA Right of Way Certified, MBCR Railroad Safety Certified and AMTRAK Safety Certified. We also have an employee on staff who is Hazardous Waste Certified. We can proudly say that we have a 30 year 100% Safety record.
